Sensoril® is the only root-and-leaf ashwagandha extract with clinical evidence specific to menopause. A recent study published in the Journal of Menopausal Medicine demonstrated that Sensoril improved menopause-related quality of life (MenQol), including vasomotor, psychosocial, physical and sexual domains.

Additionally, Sensoril was shown to support a healthy bone turnover balance, with significant decreases in bone resorption markers and increases in bone formation markers, at doses as low as 250 mg/day.

Health Edge Newsletter

Headline sugerida🟡 Sugestão — Validar com Andrea
Beyond Symptom Relief: New Data on Sensoril® Ashwagandha and Menopause Bone Health
Corpo do artigo🟡 Sugestão — Validar com Andrea
The conversation around menopause has evolved significantly — and so has the science.

When Sensoril® ashwagandha was first studied in the context of menopause, the focus was on quality of life: vasomotor symptoms, mood, physical wellbeing. The 2025 publication in the Journal of Menopausal Medicine established a strong foundation.

Now, new analysis expands that picture. Published in Gynecological and Reproductive Endocrinology and Metabolism, the latest findings confirm that Sensoril's improvements in menopause-related quality of life are not just statistically meaningful — they are clinically relevant. Crucially, the data also reveals a significant positive effect on bone turnover biomarkers: decreased resorption markers and increased formation markers, indicative of a healthier bone metabolism balance.

This matters because up to 20% of bone loss can occur during the menopausal transition, contributing to a long-term risk of osteoporosis — a condition that affects 1 in 10 women over 60 worldwide.

For health and nutrition professionals, this dual-action profile — menopause symptom support and bone health — positions Sensoril® as a uniquely relevant ingredient for the fast-growing women's health supplement category.

With 47 million women entering menopause each year by 2030, and 80% experiencing related symptoms, the market opportunity is clear. The science now provides the differentiation.

Sensoril® is available at doses as low as 250 mg/day.

Regras de Aplicação de Marca

✅ Uso correto

  • Valentia Slate (#005776) sempre presente na peça
  • Logo Kerry positivo em fundo claro; reversed (branco) em fundo escuro ou imagem
  • Headlines em Title Case; body copy em Sentence case
  • Impact statements em ALL CAPS somente com Noto Sans Condensed
  • Noto Serif apenas em aspas e citações diretas
  • IBM Plex Mono apenas para dados científicos e valores técnicos

❌ Proibido

  • Distorcer, rotacionar ou recolorir o logo Kerry
  • Logo em fundo de baixo contraste ou imagem busy
  • IBM Plex Mono em headlines, subheadings ou copy geral
  • Noto Sans Condensed em parágrafos corridos ou body copy
  • Impact statements no meio de uma frase — somente início/fim
  • Alterar cores fora da paleta oficial sem aprovação
